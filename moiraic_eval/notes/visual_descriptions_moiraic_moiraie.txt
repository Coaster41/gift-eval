Coen's summary:
Moiraic is smoother and has more (too) confident quantiles, it also often regresses to the average value missing the shape or noisiness of the data.
Moiraie is noisier and follows irregular time series shapes better, but this leads to hallucinated features at incorrect times. It is also has wider quantiles and often distinct patch artifacts.
